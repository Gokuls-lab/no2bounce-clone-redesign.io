Primary Services

‍1. Email Validation: Locate and verify email addresses efficiently to ensure messages reach real inboxes. This helps businesses maintain accurate contact data and reduces the risk of sending emails to invalid or inactive addresses.

‍2. Bulk Email Validation: Validate large email lists quickly and reliably. This service boosts deliverability, reduces bounce rates, and ensures marketing or sales campaigns are more effective.

‍3. Catch-All Email Verification: Identify domains that accept all email addresses (catch-all domains). This allows users to assess the risk of sending to such addresses and make informed decisions to protect sender reputation.

4. Email Scoring: Assess the quality and deliverability potential of any email address. Each email is assigned a score or status, helping teams prioritize safe, high-quality contacts for outreach.

5. Email Finder: Locate the email address of a specific contact with accuracy and precision. This tool is ideal for sales, marketing, and recruitment teams who need verified addresses for outreach.

‍6. Email Cleaning: Remove invalid, risky, disposable, or inactive emails from your lists. Regular cleaning improves campaign performance, reduces bounce rates, and maintains sender reputation.

7. Real-Time Email Verification API: Instantly verifies email addresses during form submissions, signups, or CRM imports to prevent bad data entry.

8. Syntax, Domain & SMTP Validation: Checks email format, domain existence, MX records, and SMTP server responses.

Secondary Services

‍1. Disposable & Role-Based Email Detection: Detects temporary or disposable email addresses (e.g., mailinator.com) and role-based emails (e.g., info@, sales@, admin@). This helps prevent invalid or non-personal addresses from entering your lists, ensuring higher engagement and protecting your sender reputation.

2. Spam Trap Risk Analysis: Identifies email addresses that may be spam traps or high-risk contacts. By avoiding these addresses, businesses can reduce the chance of blacklisting and maintain strong email deliverability.

‍3. API & Webhook Support: Enables seamless integration with applications, websites, CRMs, and marketing tools. Users can validate emails in real time or bulk directly within their existing workflows for instant and automated email verification.

‍4. CRM & Marketing Integrations: no2bounce connects with popular CRM and marketing automation platforms, such as Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, and others. This ensures that contact data is continuously cleaned, verified, and ready for outreach without manual intervention.
