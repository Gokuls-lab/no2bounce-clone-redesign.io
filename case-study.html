The Challenge

As a trusted B2B data provider serving thousands of businesses worldwide, maintaining high email data quality standards was critical — but increasingly difficult at scale.

1
Slow Validation Speed
Processing 47M+ emails took 72+ hours, creating bottlenecks and delaying time-sensitive campaigns.
2
Catch-All Email Uncertainty
15% of their database was marked as “unknown” due to catch-all domains, resulting in riskier campaigns and lost confidence.
3
Inconsistent Accuracy
Small validation errors multiplied at scale, increasing spam complaints, bounces, and inboxing issues.
4
Client Deliverability Issues
Client bounce rates rose by 12–15%, damaging sender reputation and increasing support requests by 40%.
